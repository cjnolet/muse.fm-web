In-person group listening synergy.

Historically, live music and public radio have always brought us a sense of togetherness. Recorded music was no exception until the age of the internet. Digital music and streaming internet radio has since become a very isolated experience. Personal taste profiles cater to only a single user at a time, making it hard for groups and communities to share an energizing experience together. muse.fm creates a musical experience that enhances the togetherness for groups of individuals.

Live

Current status and inspirational profile are used to impact live group listening whether for streaming radio, live DJ, or AM/FM radio broadcast.

Intelligent

muse.fm learns as you use it. All feedback is used to optimize group interactions and add impact to your listening inspirations.

Collaborative

muse.fm is social. Interact with those around you. Share inspirations, discover new music and shape your musical experience.

Music Experience

Connect muse.fm to all your music services and enhance your inspirational profile through all interactions- both personal and with the community.
