muse.fm Research

Learning to empower and inspire

Our Goals:

  • 1. To build an up-to-date dataset for researchers to be able to study the tastes of listeners across many different dimensions.

  • 2. To increase enjoyment in discovering new music and better customize listening experiences to settings and moods.

  • 3. To release a dataset and maintain a public to further innovation in the field of machine learning for user empowerment.

How can you help us?

muse.fm can use your help in promoting our studies of profiling musical tastes and customizing listening experiences to your personal lifestyle habits.

Please take a moment and provide your tastes and listening habits by connecting your Spotify and Last.fm accounts below.

Connecting your accounts will also enter you in a drawing to win a $200 Amazon gift card!



Privacy Notice: No personally identifiable information (such as usernames, emails, or contact information) will ever be shared with any other parties. All usernames will be changed when this dataset is released to the public.
