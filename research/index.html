Our Goals:

  • To build an up-to-date dataset enabling researchers to study the tastes of vastly different listeners.

  • To help users discover new music and customize listening experiences to settings, moods, and habits.

  • To publicly release and maintain an open dataset supporting further innovationation in personalized music recommendation.

Would you like to help?

muse.fm can use your help in promoting our studies of profiling musical tastes and customizing listening experiences to your personal lifestyle habits.

You can help by providing your tastes and listening habits. This can be done by connecting your Spotify and Last.fm accounts using the links above.

It only takes a moment. Every profile helps.
